If you have OpenGL installed, this code will run on your PC without any issues.

Press 'm'(eng) button on your keyboard. Morning scene will appear (static)

Press 'e'(eng) button on your keyboard. Evening scene will appear (static)

Press 'l'(eng) button on your keyboard. Loop scene will appear (dynamic)
